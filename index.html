Miao Liu (刘淼)

I am a Ph.D. student in Robotics at Georgia Tech, advised by Prof. James Rehg.

Before I joined Georgia Tech, I earned my M.S. from Carnegie Mellon University and my B.S. from Beihang University.My research interests lie at the intersection between Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Robotics. Especially, I'm interested in understanding human attention and human actions from First Person Vision Prospective.
