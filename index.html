Miao Liu (刘淼)

I am a Ph.D. student in Robotics at Georgia Tech, advised by Prof. James Rehg. I also work closely with Prof.Yin Li from University of Wisconsin−Madison. I'm fortunate to work with Prof. Siyu Tang and Prof. Michael Black during my visit at ETH Zurich and Max Planck Institute. I'm currently interning at Facebook Reality Lab, where I'm collaborating with Dr. Chao Li, and Dr. Kiran Somasundaram on 4D egocentric action recognition. Before I joined Georgia Tech, I earned my Master degree from Carnegie Mellon University and Bachelor degree from Beihang University.

My research interests lie at the intersection between Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Robotics. Especially, I'm interested in understanding human attention and human actions from First Person Vision prospective.
